Uttrakhand Association of Singapore

Uttarakhand Association of Singapore (UASG) is a non-profit voluntary organization established in Singapore. UAS's mission is to nurture a strong network of Indian diaspora around the rich and diverse heritage of Uttarakhand, India. The mission also includes to develop and implement meaningful programs in Uttarakhand state of India.

Uttrakhand - The Dev Bhoomi


Uttarakhand is a vibrant young state nestled in the lap of serene Himalayan mountain range. Uttarakhand popularly known as Dev Bhoomi - the land of Gods being the home of “Char Dham“. Its charms are muti-faceted. Here the legend of Jim Corbett lives on in the famed tiger reserve that bears his name; popular holiday towns like Mussoorie and Nainital were once Raj-era hill stations; the Beatles turned Rishikesh into a magnet for yoga practitioners worldwide; and Kainchi ashram has attracted spiritual seekers and tech titans such as Steve Jobs and Mark Zuckerberg. Many well-known personalities like Mahendra Singh Dhoni and General Bipin Rawat, Chief of Army Staff, Indian Army have their roots here. Most important attraction of this region are its simple, hardworking and soft natured welcoming residents.
